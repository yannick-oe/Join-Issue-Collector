Scope of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Join Issue Collector application, including the Kanban board, user login, stakeholder request pages and the email-based issue collection workflow.


Personal Data Processed

Depending on how the application is used, the following personal data may be processed:

  • name, initials or profile information of internal users,
  • email address of stakeholders submitting requests,
  • task titles, task descriptions, categories, priorities, deadlines and status information,
  • creator information, such as whether a task was created internally or externally,
  • email metadata such as sender address, subject, body content and submission time,
  • technical data required to provide the application, such as browser, device and usage information.

Purpose of Processing

Personal data is processed for the following purposes:

  • to provide and operate the Kanban board,
  • to create, display, update and manage tasks,
  • to allow stakeholders to submit feature requests or issues by email,
  • to analyze incoming requests and create structured tickets,
  • to assign requests to the correct board column, category and priority,
  • to send confirmation, error or status update emails,
  • to prevent excessive automated usage through a daily request limit,
  • to present the project as part of a public developer portfolio.

Email-Based Issue Collection

If a stakeholder submits a request by email, the sender address, subject and email content may be processed automatically. The email content may be used to generate a ticket in the Join board.

The system may send confirmation emails, error notifications or status update emails to the original sender. If the daily request limit is reached, no additional AI-generated ticket will be created automatically and the request may be handled manually.


AI-Supported Processing

Incoming email requests may be analyzed with AI support to determine a suitable task title, category, priority, deadline and description. AI-generated content is used only to support ticket creation and project demonstration.

AI-generated tickets should be reviewed by a human before being treated as final business decisions. No automated decision-making with legal or similarly significant effects takes place within this demo project.


Legal Basis

The processing of personal data is based on the following legal bases under Article 6 GDPR:

  • Article 6(1)(b) GDPR where processing is necessary to provide requested application functionality or respond to a submitted request.
  • Article 6(1)(f) GDPR based on the legitimate interest in operating, securing and demonstrating a functional portfolio project and issue collection system.
  • Article 6(1)(a) GDPR where consent is requested for optional processing activities.

Storage and Service Providers

The application may use Firebase to store users, contacts and tasks. n8n may be used to automate email processing and ticket creation. Email services may be used to receive and send request-related messages. AI services may be used to analyze incoming email requests.

These providers process data only to the extent necessary to provide the described functionality. Where required, suitable data processing agreements or equivalent safeguards should be used.


Local Storage

The application may use local storage in the browser to keep session-related information or user state available during use of the application. This data is stored locally on the user's device and can be deleted through the browser settings.


Data Transfers

Depending on the technical service provider used, personal data may be processed outside Austria or outside the European Economic Area. Where this happens, appropriate safeguards under the GDPR, such as standard contractual clauses or an adequacy decision, should be used where required.


Retention

Personal data is stored only for as long as necessary for the operation, testing and demonstration of the application. Demo data, test users, submitted requests and generated tickets may be deleted when they are no longer required.

Email-based requests and related task data are retained only as long as needed for processing, demonstration, troubleshooting or documentation purposes.
